The online home of frogfather.

I'm re-embracing the homespun static website life. My first site was written in self-taught HTML on the old warwick.ac.uk domain and I spent days adding things and forgetting to chmod them to +755(?) so they didn't actually appear where I put them until I could get back to the computer lab. Happier days.

Now everyone* spends their time refreshing a feed on one of three websites and the world is immeasurably worse for the lack of some idiot putting up a mouse-drawn .jpg of something called "Chipolata Monkeys" to amuse the four friends who ever logged into the site.

*some people apparently don't spend their entire waking life on their phones and I am jealous and want to get back to it

I might add more pages over time, but the main thing is: this is here.
